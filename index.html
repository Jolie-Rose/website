About Jolie Rose

Jolie Rose was born from a desire to bring the magic of diamond jewellery to every woman who dares to dream. Each piece begins as a vision — a story waiting to be told in gold and light.

We believe that fine jewellery should be both exceptional and honest. That is why every piece in our collection features certified lab-grown diamonds — real diamonds, created ethically, with the same fire and brilliance as their mined counterparts.

Our Craft

With over 20 years of expertise, our master craftsmen bring each design to life with extraordinary attention to detail. Every stone is selected for its quality, every setting handcrafted to perfection. The result is jewellery that feels as special as the moment it commemorates.

Our Promise

Every Jolie Rose piece comes with an official IGI certificate, confirming the quality and authenticity of your diamond. We offer free worldwide shipping and a personal service that ensures your experience is as precious as the jewel itself.

Contact Us

We’d love to hear from you. Whether you have a question about a piece, need help choosing a gift, or would like to discuss a custom design, our team is here to help.
