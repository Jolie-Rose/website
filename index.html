Why Jolie Rose

Lab-grown diamonds.
Real brilliance.
Honest prices.

Lab-grown diamonds are chemically, physically, and optically identical to mined diamonds — the only difference is their origin. By growing diamonds in a controlled environment, we can offer the same exceptional quality at a fraction of the price.

Every Jolie Rose piece is crafted with IGI-certified lab-grown diamonds, set in 14-karat gold and designed exclusively in-house. With over 20 years of industry expertise, we know exactly what exceptional looks like — and we make it accessible to everyone.

Our Founder
Founder & Designer
Lovely to meet you! I started Jolie Rose after years as a financial manager at a diamond company in Israel, I simply fell in love with diamonds — which led me to start designing jewellery for myself. The compliments poured in, which made me dream of becoming a jewellery designer.

When we moved to Belgium, I decided to make that dream a reality. But I wanted to be accessible to everyone — great prices without ever compromising on quality or special designs. After four years of deep research, I arrived at the perfect result: a combination of quality and accessible pricing for every budget. Today I work with carefully selected manufacturers. I design most pieces myself, so you won't find my jewellery anywhere else except right here.
